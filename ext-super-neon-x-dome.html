Super Neon X Dome is the next generation complete lighting solution that offers a 180° viewing angle that easily bends against its line of light (opposite bend to Super Neon Edge). Designed to create unlimited runs of continuous wide viewing angles of smooth “hotspot” free illumination. Ultra flexible, this next generation version of our Neon X product bends easily and is free from any form of shape memory. Create endless contours, forms, lines, curves and shapes of powerful solid light. The encapsulated curved lens gives side and front illumination offering the specifier a multitude of design options. Transparent ends allows for unlimited run lengths, combined with integral “no tool” connectors and simple leader/jumper cables gives Super Neon X Dome a rapid installation. Smart clip fixings or aluminum extrusions with a clever integral grip system completes any project. Ideal for architectural building contours, status & form details, columns, steps, walls, direct view, indirect view, signage and an infinite of applications restrained only by your imagination. Part of the Super Neon family of products.
