Aquaflex is a wet location flexible linear system that uses special grade silicone rubber housing. The housing is designed for a long life span by providing non-tear durability, extreme low and high temperature immunity and consistent color filters in order to avoid discoloration. Aquaflex arrives with up to 158 lm/ft and is field cuttable for easy installation.
