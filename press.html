MODA LIGHT® HONORED TO BE NAMED IN INC. 5000 FASTEST-GROWING COMPANIES IN AMERICA

August 2017



Las Vegas, NV — Moda Light, an award winning lighting manufacturer. Founded in 2006 by Mark Samson, Moda Light has continuously innovated in the field of specification grade LED lighting technology and high end product design. Moda Light has been recognized on the 2017, Inc. 5000 list as one of America’s fastest growing private companies.

The Inc. 5000 is ranked accordingly to percentage revenue growth over a three-year period and only a fraction of the nation’s institutions are able to demonstrate such outstanding growth. Moda Light is recognized for delivering Industry Leading, forward thinking, Linear SSL products combined with advanced firmware and driver technology. Moda Light identifies itself as an SSL hardware manufacturer and a software technology company that delivers results with longevity, ease of use, and creative design aesthetics.

“Moda is honored to be named on the Inc. 5000 list. This is recognition for many years of hard work and a focus on a ‘technology first’ approach” said Mark Samson, CEO of Moda Light. “It is evidence of the commitment and strategy of the Moda team, who is focused on delivering the best means of illumination and exceptional service to all our customers.”

About Moda Light

Moda Light, an award winning manufacturer, was formed in 2006 by Mark Samson who has over 34 years of experience in the Lighting and SSL industry. Presently located in the 24-hour city, Las Vegas, Nevada, we stand prominently in the SSL industry, thriving on the challenges of things considered “impossible”. We create truly new and original lighting technology, manipulating and inventing new hardware and software to produce what was once considered inaccessible data from LED products.


About Inc. 5000

The 2017 Inc. 5000 list ranks companies according to percentage revenue growth between 2013 and 2016. To qualify, companies must be U.S.-based, privately held, for profit and independent, and must have been founded and generating revenue by March 31, 2013. Companies on the Inc. 5000 are featured in Inc.’s September issue. They represent the top tier of the Inc. 5000 and can be found at www.inc.com.
