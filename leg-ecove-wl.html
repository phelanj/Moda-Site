E-Cove is a cost effective cove lighting system. Powerful, delivering 480 lm/ft and discreet with a height of just 1 7/10” (43.6mm), E-Cove is ideal to illuminate coves, alcoves and niches. Advanced design, means that this fixture runs incredibly cool even after months of continuous use which keeps phosphor degradation to a minimum and ensures the fixture has a healthy and reliable L70.

The onboard Moda designed American Dimming Chipset, programmed by our software division with new advanced firmware ensures E-Cove delivers smooth, no noise, flicker free and synchronized dimming down to 0% with ELV dimmers. Unlike other fixtures in the marketplace there is no need to purchase additional adapters, convertors, dimming boxes or control modules, just use conventional wiring direct from the dimmer to create a powerful lighting system, saving time and money. With no minimum run lengths, just one 1ft fixture will dim seamlessly to 0%. ..

If the installation requires 0-10V control then utilizing Moda Bridge S1 between the dimmer and lighting system - this smart device will convert a 0-10V signal into an ELV signal which increases the multitude of projects that E-Cove can benefit and enhance. Our Bridge has been carefully designed to work with all of our ELV lighting fixtures.

Achieve beautiful cove illumination with E-Cove with no color over angle, no striations and no dark spots. Long single runs of up to 230 feet (277V) decreases the number of wiring circuits required in an installation. Install in almost any installation with it’s built-in 100-277V 50/60 Hz power supply.

With the ability to mix our own phosphors we are able to make a revolutionary new standard for Moda offering a High Color Rendering Index of 96-98 with LEDs centered on The Planckian Locus for superior illumination. R1-R15 tested to TM30-15 with values in excess of 90, creating beautiful cove illumination. Single binning means every fixture will have the same CCT - something that Moda has been doing since 2006. Choose from our standard white light temperatures of 2200K, 2700K, 3000K, 3500K & 4000K.

Integral locking bracket which rotates 170 degrees for almost any light direction that is required. Built-in male & female connectors with thumb latch connections for fast installation time. Lightweight, tough 5VA polycarbonate construction for durability and an excellent white aesthetic finish. Quality, American made electronic components.

Tested by Intertek to UL1598 for use in USA & Canada. CE Approved. TM-30-15 Tested. RoHS Compliant.

Designed & Developed by Moda Light in Las Vegas, USA.
