Graze RS is a high quality graze lighting solution designed for fast installation with its integral thumb latch push to fit Kwik Connect connectors. 0-10V synchronized dimming down to 10% with a High CRI of 93 and R1-R15 values in the high nineties. Brushed aluminum body, houses the spec ready fixture which boasts interlocking fields of light, no striations and no color over angle. Integral power supply can accept 100-277V. Rotates 180° for varying light direction. Choose from Standard Output (SO) which has a lumen output of 455 lm/ft or High Output (HO) which has a lumen output of 1236 lm/ft. Beam angles available are 6°, 10°, 25°, 40°, 10°x40°, 30°x60°, 30°x60°x5°.
