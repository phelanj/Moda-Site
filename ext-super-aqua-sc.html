Super Aquaflex is a wet location flexible linear system with high output and a superior level of illumination. The wet location housing is cured for an extended period of time aiding in its life span. Moda Light uses wet location housing that is classified as special grade silicone rubber. This provides higher non-tear durability, extreme low and high temperature immunity and consistent color filters to avoid discoloration. Super Aquaflex reels arrive up 472 lm/ft and are field cuttable every 1” intended for easy installation. Each reel includes 600 high quality SMD LEDs and a unique high copper content FPCB design for stable and consistent thermal management. Super Aquaflex products are durable and a great choice for wet location linear applications.
